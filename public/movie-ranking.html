My top 3 Horror Movies

Mama

Very light on blood and gore, but there are lot of powerfully scary, spooky images, as well as scenes.

Hereditary

Made in 2018. It is dark, creepy horror movie with ghosts,seances, death, burned bodies, screaming and panic. Very disturbing movie but greatly done at the same time.

Annabelle

Made in 2014 and while it is not as good as that film in 1968, Annabelle is still a good time at a movies and i recommend it.
